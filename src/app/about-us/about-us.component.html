About Edonee

The present-day learning curve of the students had got dramatically enhanced and the teaching methodology has equally undergone a renaissance. The industry of today expect a lot more from students and which is much beyond what is being taught in the schools, colleges and quite lot of academic institutions which have got sprouted across the landscape.
Edonee has become a tag name in the education segment and has transformed itself to become a perfect mobile which regards itself as a prefect for students in the highly competitive education segment.
The app focuses on various methods of additional learning for students and gives them a holistic information and insights for students who are applying for their graduation and for those who are applying for their post-graduation.
The methodology that Edonee has been put in practice is to transcend the study criteria for students and helps to redesign the academic curriculum and learning methodologies and makes the students get a superior grip on the subjects and indeed helps to get into the corporate world and perform better.
